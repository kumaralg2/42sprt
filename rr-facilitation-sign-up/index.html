Rogue & Rouge Facilitation Expression of Interest Sign Up

  • Thank you from the Rogue & Rouge team! We are excited about your interest in being a Facilitator to our growing cohort of Young Masters.

    A Rogue & Rouge Facilitator or Co-Facilitator is a very special role, one that we value very highly as do our Young Masters. As a Facilitator you will draw from your love of inspiring a younger generation to be the best that they can be, guide them to connect with their own sense of brilliance that resides in all of them.

    R&R Facilitators and Co-Facilitators are encouraging, supportive and are willing to be vulnerable and real, to set an example of true leadership, support and comfortability.

    Please note that these programs require your availability during business hours as facilitation takes places within the school setting.
